Definition List

A definition list is a collection of terms and their definitions. In HTML, the <dl> element is used to create a definition list, with each term defined by a <dt> (definition term) element and its corresponding definition provided by a <dd> (definition description) element.

Definition lists are commonly used for glossaries, FAQs, or any situation where you want to present a list of terms along with their explanations or definitions.
